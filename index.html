Hot Girl Sh!t Preloved

HGS Preloved – short for Hot Girl Sh!t – is a personal project born from my love of fashion and the thrill of finding gems and one-off statement pieces. I handpick vintage and designer treasures that deserve a second life, focusing on in and everlasting fashion.

From designer icons to rare vintage finds, each item is carefully chosen for its individuality and charm – its purpose: to bring out the inner hot girl in you.
